About Us

Barrio Ca phe is not your regular cafe in the Philippines. Nothing fancy here. No indoor shop, no comfortable chairs or sofa, no fancy decorations. Instead, we leave everything to nature. The green rice field, the mountain, and the magical horizon will definitely entertain you and make you cofee break comfortable while your newly found favourite coffee is being made in an open cart parked on one side of the ca phe.

Located in a peaceful coutryside, Barrio San Felipe, this cafe showcases the beauty of nature alongside the taste of Vietnamese coffee.

Another unique feature of the ca phe isit also encourages its customers to bring out their creativity through painting ceramics.

Barrio sunset ca phe will definitely provide you a special experience.
